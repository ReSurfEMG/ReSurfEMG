Medical Professional Documentation¶

This is the present documentation home for medical professionals who do not spend most of their time coding. From here you will be able to link to our explanatory videos…under development

Introduction¶

resurfemg is a package meant to support tools for surface EMG interpretation. Surface EMG interpretation is a rapidly evolving field without many standards. We build interfaces for ongoing research in the form of Jupyter notebooks, as well as clinical tools for clinical research.
